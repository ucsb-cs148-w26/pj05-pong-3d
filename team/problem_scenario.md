# Problem Scenario

Kobby, 14, is a student in a California high school.
He is bored during his lunch break and needs something on his Chromebook to pass the time.
He is browsing the web for unblocked games on his school network, looking for a fun game that doesn't require much thought.

Alex, 70, is a retired grandfather staying at home.
He is waiting for his family to visit him later today, and is reminiscing of playing Pong on his Atari console.
His grandson Kobby recently shared a game with him that he can play to pass the time.
