# Team Agreements
## Attendance & Absences
- We will each make an effort to keep absences to a minimum.
- Team members who are present will help keep absent members informed by sharing notes, decisions, and important updates.

## Communication
- We agree to communicate clearly, respectfully, and honestly with one another.
- We will regularly check and respond to messages on agreed-upon communication platforms.

## Contribution & Responsibility
- We will all do our best to contribute meaningfully to the team's success.
- We will support each other by sharing knowledge and helping when needed.

## Team Respect & Collaboration
- We value different perspectives and understand that collaboration leads to better outcomes.
- We will listen actively and be open to feedback.

## Conflict Resolution
- If disagreements arise, we will address them calmly and respectfully within the team.
- If we are unable to resolve an issue on our own, we will seek guidance from a TA or instructor.
