# 3D Pong User Manual

3D Pong is a web-based multiplayer game that brings the classic game of Pong
into three dimensions. We intend to serve users that want to play a quick,
simple, competitive game to pass the time or socialize with others.

## Logging in

First, visit the [3D Pong website](https://pong.wongzhao.com) in a web browser.
Press the link on the screen to log in using your Google account.

## User profile

Once logged in, there will be a button to view and edit your profile at the top
of the page.

Take this time to adjust your display name, which will appear to other players
in game.

When you unlock cosmetics, you will also be able to equip them here by pressing
on the one you want to use.

Once ready to start a game, return to the main page.

## Starting a game

To start a game, fill in a lobby name on the main page and press "Create Lobby."
You will join the game as host and be shown the waiting screen.

You need one other player to play against, who you can invite using the Join
Code or tell them to join from the main page.

Once the second player joins, press "Start Game" to start the game.

## Playing the game

Move the paddle using the WASD keys. Your goal is to prevent the ball from
hitting your side by moving the paddle.

Every time someone hits the ball, it will get faster. Once the ball hits
someone's side, its speed resets.

Your and your opponent's lives are shown at the top (you on the left, opponent
on the right). The last player alive wins.
